Our Story

A Sanctuary Above the Clouds

Perched at 2,100 feet in the Sahyadri range, Serenity Hills Resort & Spa began as a dream — to create a place where the pace of city life simply dissolves. Spread across four lush acres overlooking the Mulshi valley, every corner of our property was designed to honour the land it sits on.

The architecture draws from the vernacular stone-and-timber craft of the Western Ghats, while the interiors wrap you in understated luxury. Whether you're a couple seeking a quiet weekend, a family building memories, or a team stepping away for a creative offsite — the hills have a way of giving you exactly what you need.

Culinary

Farm-to-Table

The Mulshi Table

Our restaurant is a love letter to the land. Every morning, fresh produce arrives from our organic kitchen garden and the farms of Mulshi — leafy greens still glistening with dew, heirloom tomatoes that taste like summer, and herbs picked minutes before they hit your plate.

Chef Aditi Sharma leads a team that celebrates the Western Ghats' rich culinary heritage while weaving in contemporary techniques. The seasonal menu changes every six weeks, guided by what the land offers.

Wellness

Surrender to Stillness

Our spa draws its philosophy from Ayurveda and the healing energy of the Sahyadri hills. Every treatment begins with a dosha consultation and uses cold-pressed oils, wildcrafted herbs, and mineral-rich clays sourced from the Western Ghats.

The wellness centre includes a heated indoor pool, eucalyptus steam room, yoga pavilion overlooking the valley, and six treatment suites designed for complete sensory retreat.

Adventures

Explore the Wild Side

The Sahyadri range is your playground. From gentle morning treks to adrenaline-filled kayaking on Mulshi Lake, every day holds a new adventure.

🥾

Hill Trekking

Guided trails through ancient trade routes of the Sahyadris. Choose from easy sunrise walks to full-day ridge treks with packed lunches.

🚴

Valley Cycling

Premium mountain bikes, curated routes through village roads and reservoir trails. Helmets, guides, and hydration kits provided.

🛶

Lake Kayaking
