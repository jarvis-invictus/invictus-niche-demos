Frequently Asked Questions

What room types are available at The Courtyard Inn Pune?
We offer Deluxe Rooms, Superior Suites, and a Premium Terrace Suite. All rooms feature modern amenities, high-speed WiFi, and elegant interiors. Our Koregaon Park location puts you close to the best restaurants, cafes, and nightlife that Pune has to offer.
Do you offer free airport transfer for direct bookings?
Yes, all direct bookings through our website or phone include complimentary airport transfer from Pune International Airport. The drive to our Koregaon Park hotel takes about 20 minutes. We also arrange transfers to the railway station and major business districts on request.
Is there a restaurant at The Courtyard Inn?
Our rooftop restaurant serves multi-cuisine dining with beautiful views of Koregaon Park. We offer breakfast, lunch, and dinner with both Indian and continental options. The rooftop bar is a popular evening spot for guests and locals alike, open until 11 PM.
Does the hotel have conference and meeting facilities?
Yes, we have a conference room that seats up to 40 people with projector, screen, and video conferencing equipment. Many corporate clients from Hinjewadi and Kharadi IT parks use our meeting facilities for off-site meetings. Day packages with meals are available.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in is at 2 PM and check-out is at 12 noon. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability, often at no extra charge for direct bookings. Our front desk is available 24 hours for any assistance you need.
