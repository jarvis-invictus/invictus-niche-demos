Where We Take You

From barefoot beach ceremonies in Goa to regal palace vows in Udaipur — we know every hidden corner, every reliable vendor, every magical golden hour spot.

Beach & Sun · India

Goa

Golden sand ceremonies, teal waters, and colonial Portuguese charm. Sunset vows on Vagator cliffs or intimate beach dinners under fairy lights — Goa is pure magic.

Palace & Lake · Rajasthan

Udaipur

The City of Lakes never fails to steal hearts. Lake-floating mandaps, heritage haveli courtyards, and candlelit dinners where royalty once feasted — this is a wedding and a fairy tale both.

Hills & Mist · Maharashtra

Lonavala

Just two hours from Pune, yet worlds away. Misty hilltop ceremonies, jungle resort exclusives, and that cool monsoon air that makes every photograph breathtaking and every moment feel intimate.

International · Bali · Thailand · Maldives

International

Bali's clifftop temples. Thailand's island sunsets. The Maldives' overwater silence. We handle visas, vendor legalities, currency, and everything in between — you just show up and say "I do."

What We Do

Full-Circle
Wedding Planning

We're not just a vendor list. We're your on-the-ground team, your legal guide, your taste curator, and your biggest fans — all in one.

🗺️

Venue Scouting

We personally visit and vet shortlisted venues — from Goa beach resorts to Bali jungle retreats — so you only see spaces we'd put our own name on. Site visits, walkthroughs, video tours included.

🤝

Vendor Coordination

Caterers, florists, mehendi artists, DJs, sound engineers — our curated network of 300+ trusted vendors across all destinations means zero compromise on quality, even far from home.

✈️

Travel & Stay Management

Block bookings for your entire guest list, airport transfers, welcome kits, and room allocation — all coordinated. Your guests land stress-free and leave raving about the experience.

🌸

Décor & Floral Design

Dusty rose and champagne gold tablescapes. Suspended floral installations. Moonlit mandaps. Our in-house décor team collaborates with you on every aesthetic reference and translates dreams into visuals.

📸

Photography & Film Booking

We connect you with destination-specialist photographers who understand light, culture, and storytelling. From cinematic drone shots to candid film rolls — your wedding becomes an album worth decades.

📋

Legal & Documentation

International marriage registration, visa letters for guests, local authority permits, venue contracts in local languages — we've done this dozens of times and make it completely painless for you.
