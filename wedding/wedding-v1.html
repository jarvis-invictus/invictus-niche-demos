Our Process

Three Simple Steps to
Your Dream Wedding

We've refined our process over 350+ weddings so the journey feels as magical as the day itself.

1

Share Your Vision

We start with an intimate consultation — over chai, of course. Share your dreams, your aesthetic, your traditions. We listen to every detail and capture the essence of your story.

2

We Design & Plan

Our team curates venues, designs décor concepts, coordinates vendors, crafts timelines, and prepares every detail — delivering a comprehensive plan you'll fall in love with.

3

Celebrate in Style

On the big day, our crew handles everything behind the scenes. You simply walk in, soak in the beauty, and celebrate your love surrounded by the people who matter most.
