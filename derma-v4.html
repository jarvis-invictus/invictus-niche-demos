Specialty Conditions

Chronic Conditions We Actually Resolve

These conditions often have root causes in gut health, immunity, and inflammation — which is why conventional treatments alone often don't work long-term.

Chronic
Psoriasis — Root Cause Approach
Beyond the steroid creams. We investigate gut dysbiosis, leaky gut, gluten sensitivity, and stress pathways that perpetuate psoriatic flares. Biologics used only when needed.
Common root causes: leaky gut, Streptococcal infection, gluten sensitivity, stress
Chronic
Eczema & Atopic Dermatitis
Investigating food triggers (dairy, gluten, eggs), gut microbiome disruption, and skin barrier genetics alongside topical and systemic treatment.
Common root causes: food allergies, dysbiosis, environmental triggers, stress
Hormonal
Hormonal & Adult Acne
Full hormonal workup — androgens, cortisol, insulin resistance, PCOS markers — alongside targeted dermatological and nutritional intervention.
Common root causes: insulin resistance, androgen excess, gut-skin axis disruption
Autoimmune
Vitiligo — Integrative Protocol
Combining NBUVB phototherapy with antioxidant nutrition, stress management, and immune-modulating supplementation for improved repigmentation outcomes.
Common root causes: oxidative stress, autoimmune dysregulation, nutrient deficiencies
Our Doctor

Meet Dr. Meera Kulkarni

👩‍⚕️
Dr. Meera Kulkarni
MD Dermatology · Functional Medicine Practitioner
🎓 MBBS – BJMC Pune 🎓 MD DVL – KEM Hospital, Mumbai 🌿 Certified Functional Medicine (IFM) 📚 Fellowship: Gut-Skin Axis, Amsterdam 📋 11 years clinical + functional practice

Dr. Meera began her career as a conventional dermatologist at KEM Hospital, Mumbai — but after years of watching patients with psoriasis, eczema, and cystic acne cycle through treatments that never truly resolved their conditions, she began investigating deeper.

She pursued certification in Functional Medicine through the Institute for Functional Medicine (IFM) and studied the gut-skin axis at AMC in Amsterdam. Over a decade later, she has developed one of Pune's most comprehensive approaches to chronic skin conditions.

"Most of my psoriasis patients were told they'd be on biologics for life. Half of them are now in remission. The difference was going after root causes instead of just suppressing symptoms."
