What We Offer

Crafted for Your Day

Every wedding is a universe of its own. From the haldi-stained laughter at dawn to the last dance under fairy lights, we document every chapter with care and intention.

Wedding Day Coverage

Full-day documentation from the mehendi ceremony to the vidaai — two lead photographers and an assistant capturing parallel moments across venues like The Corinthians, Oxford Golf Resort, and intimate Koregaon Park bungalows.

Pre-Wedding Shoots

Cinematic couple sessions at Pune's most romantic locations — Aga Khan Palace at golden hour, the cobbled lanes of Kasba Peth, Lavasa's lakefront, or the Western Ghats. Half-day and full-day options available.

Candid Photography

Unscripted, unposed, unforgettable. Our candid approach captures the in-between moments — a grandmother's blessing, cousins dancing off-beat, the groom's first look. The real stuff that makes you cry years later.

Luxury Albums

Handcrafted flush-mount albums with Italian leatherette covers and archival-grade paper. Designed in-house with a cinematic editorial layout. Available in 12×18 and 10×14 sizes with custom embossing.

Video Highlights

A 4-8 minute cinematic highlight film edited to music that matches your story's rhythm. Shot on mirrorless cinema rigs with drone aerials — perfect for sharing on Instagram or playing at the reception.

Destination Weddings

From Goa's beachfront mandaps to Udaipur's palace courtyards, we travel wherever your love takes you. Destination packages include travel, two shooters, and a same-day edit teaser for your social feeds.

The Photographer

Arjun Deshpande

Founder & Lead Photographer

I picked up my first camera at 16 — a battered Canon AE-1 from Juna Bazaar — and never looked back. What started as a fascination with light and shadow during college at Fergusson turned into a full-time obsession with documenting human connection.

ShutterVows was born in 2018 from a tiny studio above a German Bakery café in Koregaon Park. Seven years and over 300 weddings later, our team of five photographers and two cinematographers still operates from the same lane, fuelled by cutting chai and an unwavering belief that every love story deserves to be told with honesty and artistry.

Our work has been featured in WeddingSutra, Canvera's Top 50, and Wedding Nama. But the recognition we value most? When a bride's mother calls us months later to say the photos made her cry all over again.
