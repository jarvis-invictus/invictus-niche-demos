Safety First — Always

Your baby's safety is our non-negotiable promise

Every prop, every pose, every moment in our studio is designed with your newborn's wellbeing as the absolute priority.

Temperature-Controlled Studio

Our home studio in Aundh is maintained at 26–28°C — the ideal temperature for undressed newborns. A dedicated heater runs throughout sessions, and we monitor temperature continuously.

Certified Newborn Safety Training

Priya has completed advanced newborn posing safety courses with international accreditation. Every composite pose is created in post — we never place your baby in an unsupported position.

Sanitised Props & Fabrics

All wraps, blankets, baskets, and props are freshly laundered with hypoallergenic detergent before every single session. We use hospital-grade surface sanitiser on all hard surfaces.

⏱

Baby-Led Timing

Sessions are never rushed. We allow 2–4 hours so your baby can feed, sleep, and be comforted as needed. Parents are always within arm's reach, and a spotter is present for every pose.

Vaccination & Wellness Policy

All team members stay up to date on vaccinations. If anyone is feeling unwell, we reschedule at no charge. Your baby's immune system comes first — no exceptions.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

First-time parents have lots of questions. We love answering every single one.

We recommend booking during your second trimester (around 28–32 weeks). This ensures we reserve your estimated due date on our calendar. Don't worry about the exact date — we always keep flexibility for early or late arrivals and will schedule your actual session once baby is here, ideally between day 5 and 14.
We can absolutely still photograph your baby! The 5–14 day window is ideal because babies sleep deeply and curl naturally. After 21 days, we shift to a more lifestyle-focused approach with awake, alert baby portraits that are equally beautiful — just a different style. We adapt to your baby, always.
Plan for 2–4 hours, though the actual shooting time is often shorter. The extra time accounts for feeding breaks, diaper changes, soothing, and settling. We never rush — your baby sets the pace. Parents are welcome to relax with chai or coffee while we work our magic.
We provide all wraps, blankets, baskets, and props — freshly sanitised for each session. Just bring your baby (fed and in a loose outfit), extra diapers, wipes, a change of clothes, your feeding supplies, a pacifier if you use one, and any sentimental items you'd like included (a special toy, heirloom blanket, etc.).
Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. Many of the "poses" you see in our portfolio are actually composites — two images merged together in editing. Your baby is never placed in any position without full head and body support. A trained spotter's hands are always within centimetres of your baby, even when you can't see them in the final image.
We completely understand that life with a newborn is unpredictable. Rescheduling is free with 24 hours notice. If baby or anyone in the family is unwell on session day, we reschedule at no charge — no questions asked. Your booking deposit of ₹3,000 is non-refundable but is fully applied to your package.
