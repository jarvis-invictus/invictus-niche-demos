The Photographer

I'm Ananya.
I Tell Stories
With Light.

"Photography is not about the camera — it's about the quiet moment before the smile, the look exchanged across a room."

Born and raised in Pune, I discovered my love for portraiture in a dimly lit film lab at 19. Today, with over 9 years behind the lens, I've had the privilege of documenting over 850 families, couples, and individuals across Maharashtra — each session a collaboration, each image a shared memory.

My studio in Viman Nagar was built with one intention: to give every client a space where they feel completely seen. No rush. No posing guides. Just real people, honest light, and the magic that happens when you stop trying to look perfect.
