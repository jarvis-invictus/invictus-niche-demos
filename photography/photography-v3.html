How It Works

From Inquiry to Inbox in 4 Steps

We've streamlined our workflow so you spend zero time in the studio and maximum time selling. Most shoots complete within one business day.

01

Brief & Quote

Share your product details, marketplace requirements, and reference images. We send a fixed-price quote within 2 hours — no surprise charges, ever.

02

Ship or Drop Off

Send your products to our Viman Nagar studio via Dunzo, Porter, or drop them off yourself. We handle everything from unboxing to inventory tracking.

03

Shoot & Retouch

Our team photographs each product under controlled studio lighting, then runs every image through our retouching pipeline — clipping, color grading, and format export.

04

Deliver & Ship Back

Download your images from a private Google Drive link within 24–48 hours. Products are safely packed and shipped back to your location across Pune.
