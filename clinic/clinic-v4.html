Your Journey With Us

From Conception to Cradle

Every milestone of your pregnancy, guided with expertise and warmth. Here's how we walk this beautiful path together.

🌱
Weeks 1–12 · First Trimester

The Beautiful Beginning

Your first visit! We confirm the pregnancy, check vitals, run essential blood work, and plan your personalized care. We answer every nervous question — there are no silly ones here.

🌸
Weeks 13–16 · Growing Strong

First Movements & Scans

The anomaly scan, NT scan, and double marker test. We monitor baby's growth and your health. This is when many moms first feel those magical flutters!

🦋
Weeks 17–28 · Second Trimester

The Golden Period

Energy returns, baby bump shows! Regular check-ups, growth scans, and GD screening. We discuss birth plans, nutrition, and exercise — you're glowing!

🌙
Weeks 29–36 · Third Trimester

Almost There

Fortnightly visits, NST monitoring, and birth readiness. We prepare you for delivery — breathing techniques, what to pack, when to come in. Your support system is ready.

👶
Weeks 37–40 · The Big Day

Welcome to the World!

Weekly visits, delivery planning, and the moment you've been waiting for. We're right beside you — calm, skilled, and genuinely excited for you. Post-delivery care and breastfeeding support included.
