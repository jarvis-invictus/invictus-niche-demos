Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about functional & integrative medicine

What is functional medicine and how is it different from regular treatment? ＋

Functional medicine focuses on finding and treating the root cause of health issues rather than just managing symptoms. At Integra Health Centre, consultations are 45–60 minutes long, involving comprehensive lab work, detailed history, and personalised protocols combining nutrition, lifestyle changes, and targeted supplementation.

What are the consultation timings and fees at Integra Health Centre? ＋

We are open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, by appointment only. The first consultation fee is ₹1,200 for a 60-minute deep assessment. Call +91 98765 00004 to book your slot.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in? ＋

Integra Health Centre operates by appointment only to ensure each patient gets dedicated 45–60 minute consultations. Please call +91 98765 00004 to schedule your visit.

What conditions does the clinic specialise in treating? ＋

We specialize in gut health (IBS, SIBO, leaky gut), hormonal imbalance (PCOD, thyroid, adrenal fatigue), chronic fatigue, brain fog, unexplained weight gain, metabolic syndrome, and preventive health optimization.

Does the clinic accept health insurance for functional medicine consultations? ＋

We provide detailed medical bills, lab reports, and consultation records that can be submitted for insurance reimbursement. Coverage depends on your specific insurance policy. Please check with your provider regarding functional medicine consultations.
