🌈 What We Offer

Comprehensive Care for Growing Kids

From their very first breath to their first steps into adulthood — we're here at every milestone.

👶

Newborn Care

Expert care from day one — breastfeeding support, jaundice management, weight monitoring, and newborn screening to give your baby the best start.

💉

Vaccinations

Complete immunization as per IAP & National Immunization Schedule. Painless vaccines available. We track every dose so you never miss one.

📈

Developmental Milestones

Regular developmental screening for motor, speech, cognitive and social milestones. Early identification means early support for your child.

🏥

NICU Follow-Up

Specialized follow-up for preterm and NICU graduates — growth tracking, neurodevelopmental assessment, and catch-up care plans.

🥗

Nutrition & Diet

Age-appropriate nutrition guidance, weaning advice, managing picky eaters, and addressing growth concerns with personalized diet plans.

🎒

School Health

School readiness assessments, vision & hearing checks, fitness certificates, and managing common childhood illnesses that affect school days.
