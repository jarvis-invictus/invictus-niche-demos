Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Rajesh Kulkarni

MBBS, MD (General Medicine)

Born and raised in Pune, Dr. Rajesh Kulkarni started Aashirwad Family Clinic in 2011 with a simple belief: good healthcare doesn't need to be complicated or impersonal.

After completing his MBBS from B.J. Medical College, Pune and his MD from Sassoon Hospital, he chose to set up practice right here in Kothrud — the neighbourhood he grew up in. For him, treating patients isn't just about prescriptions. It's about understanding their lives, their stresses, and their families.

"Half the treatment is just listening properly," he often tells medical students who observe at the clinic. His patients — many of whom have been coming since day one — would agree.

B.J. Medical College, Pune MMC Registered IMA Member Sassoon Hospital Alumni

"I believe every patient deserves unhurried attention. When someone walks into my clinic, they're not a case number — they're a neighbour, a friend. That's how Kothrud works."

— Dr. Rajesh Kulkarni
