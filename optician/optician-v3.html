Did You Know?

Why Children Need
Regular Eye Tests

80% of what a child learns at school comes through their eyes. Don't let blurry vision become a hidden barrier to their growth.

  • 1

    Vision Problems Are Often Silent

    Most kids can't tell you their vision is blurry — it's the only world they know. A routine test catches what they can't communicate.

  • 2

    School Performance Takes a Hit

    Difficulty reading the board, headaches after homework — these are often vision signs, not attention issues.

  • 3

    Lazy Eye Is Treatable — Especially Early

    Amblyopia responds much better to treatment before age 7. The sooner we catch it, the better the outcome.

  • 4

    Screen Time Is Rising Fast

    Modern kids average 5+ hours of screens daily. Blue-light protection and myopia monitoring have never been more important.
