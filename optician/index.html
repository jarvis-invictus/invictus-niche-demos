Frequently Asked Questions

What eye care services does VisionPlus offer in Pune?
We offer comprehensive eye testing, prescription eyeglasses, contact lens fitting, kids eye exams, and glaucoma screening. Our computerized eye testing equipment provides accurate prescriptions. Patients from Deccan, Shivajinagar, and FC Road visit us for trusted eye care.
How much does an eye test cost at VisionPlus?
A comprehensive eye examination costs ₹300, which includes computerized refraction, retinal check, and eye pressure measurement. The test fee is often waived when you purchase glasses from us. We recommend annual eye check-ups for everyone, especially children and screen users.
What eyeglasses brands and frames do you carry?
We stock over 2,000 frames from brands like Ray-Ban, Oakley, Titan, Fastrack, and designer labels. Our collection ranges from ₹500 budget frames to premium options above ₹10,000. Our optometrists help you choose frames that suit your face shape and prescription needs.
Do you offer contact lens fitting and trials?
Yes, our optometrists provide professional contact lens fitting with trial lenses so you can try before you buy. We stock daily, monthly, and yearly lenses from Bausch & Lomb, Johnson & Johnson, and Alcon. First-time users get a complete wear-and-care tutorial.
Can VisionPlus detect eye diseases during a routine check-up?
Our comprehensive eye exams screen for glaucoma, cataracts, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment. If we detect anything concerning, we refer you to trusted ophthalmologists in Pune for specialized care and follow-up.
