The Founder

Meera's Story

It started with a pair of brass-framed round spectacles Meera found at a flea market in Nashik in 2007. She was 23, studying opticianry, and the frames cost her ₹80. She wore them for years.

Two years later, armed with a small loan from her mother and an obsession with mid-century design, she opened Retro Specs in a 200 sq ft room on East Street, Camp. The shop smelled of cedar and old books. It still does.

Today Meera sources frames from estate sales across Maharashtra — each piece cleaned, restored, and priced with the care of someone who knows what it means to find the right pair of glasses.

"Glasses are the most intimate thing you wear. They sit on your face, they frame how the world sees you. They deserve to have a history."

— Meera Iyer, Founder
