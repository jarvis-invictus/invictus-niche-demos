About GenePath

Where Science Meets Certainty

GenePath Advanced Diagnostics was founded with a singular mission: to bring world-class specialized testing to India. Located in the heart of Koregaon Park, Pune, our state-of-the-art laboratory houses next-generation sequencing platforms, real-time PCR systems, and advanced immunoassay analyzers.

We go beyond routine diagnostics. Our team of molecular biologists, genetic counselors, and clinical pathologists work together to decode complex medical questions — from hereditary cancer risk assessment to rare autoimmune markers that most labs simply cannot test for.

Our Specialized Services

Advanced Testing for Complex Conditions

From genomic profiling to rare disease markers, our specialized panels provide the depth of analysis that standard laboratories cannot offer.

Genetic & Genomic Testing

Comprehensive whole-exome and targeted gene panel sequencing for hereditary conditions, pharmacogenomics, and carrier screening. We decode your DNA to reveal actionable health insights with clinical-grade accuracy.

NGS-Based

Tumor Marker Panels

Advanced oncology marker panels including PSA, CA-125, CEA, AFP, CA 19-9, HE4, and emerging liquid biopsy markers. Critical for early cancer detection, treatment monitoring, and recurrence surveillance.

Oncology

Hormonal Profiles

Complete endocrine panels covering reproductive hormones, adrenal function, growth factors, and metabolic markers. Our LC-MS/MS platform delivers unmatched sensitivity for even trace hormone levels.

Endocrinology

Auto-Immune Panels

Extensive autoimmune profiling including ANA, anti-dsDNA, ANCA, anti-CCP, complement levels, and organ-specific autoantibodies. Essential for diagnosing lupus, rheumatoid arthritis, vasculitis, and over 80 autoimmune conditions.

Immunology

Thyroid Detailed Analysis

Beyond basic TSH — our comprehensive thyroid panel covers Free T3, Free T4, TSH, thyroid antibodies (TPO, TG), thyroglobulin, calcitonin, and reverse T3. Molecular testing for thyroid nodule classification available.

Advanced Panel

BRCA Screening

Full BRCA1 and BRCA2 gene sequencing with large rearrangement analysis. Includes genetic counseling, risk assessment, and specialist referral for individuals with family history of breast or ovarian cancer.

Cancer Genetics

Whole Blood Sequencing Inquiry

Comprehensive blood-based genomic analysis covering hematological conditions, thrombophilia panels, hemoglobinopathy screening, and complete blood genome profiling for diagnostic odyssey patients.

Hematology

How It Works

Your Testing Journey

From sample collection to specialist consultation, we've designed a seamless experience that respects both the science and your time.

1

Book & Consult

Schedule online or walk in. Our genetic counselor reviews your history and recommends the most appropriate test panel for your needs.

2

Sample Collection

Professional phlebotomy at our lab or home collection service. Samples are processed immediately in our temperature-controlled facility.

3

Advanced Analysis

Your sample undergoes rigorous multi-platform testing with double-validation protocols. Every result is reviewed by a senior molecular pathologist.

4

Results & Referral

Receive a detailed report with clinical interpretation within 48-72 hours. Specialist referral arranged if your results warrant further evaluation.
