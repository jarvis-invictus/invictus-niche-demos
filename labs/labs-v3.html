Our Services

Comprehensive Corporate Health Solutions

From onboarding to ongoing wellness, we cover every aspect of occupational health diagnostics with precision and reliability.

Pre-Employment Health Checkups

Comprehensive medical evaluations for new hires — including physical exams, blood work, vision tests, and drug screening. Ensure your new employees are fit for duty from day one.

Annual Health Screenings

Scheduled yearly health assessments for your entire workforce. Track employee health trends over time and identify risk factors early with our data-driven screening programs.

Employee OPD Services

On-site and in-clinic outpatient consultations for your employees. General medicine, specialist referrals, and follow-up care — all under a single corporate agreement.

COVID & Flu Testing

Rapid antigen tests, RT-PCR, and influenza panels with same-day results. Bulk testing for offices, factories, and events — helping you maintain a safe workplace environment.

Corporate Health Packages

Customizable health packages designed for organizations of any size. Choose from Basic or Comprehensive plans with volume-based pricing that fits your budget.

Ambulatory ECG Monitoring

24-48 hour Holter monitoring and ambulatory ECG services for employees with cardiac risk factors. Advanced cardiac diagnostics with cardiologist-reviewed reporting.
