Our Services

Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

From routine blood work to advanced imaging, our NABL-accredited lab delivers precise and reliable results you can trust for every health decision.

Complete Blood Count

Full haematology panel including CBC, ESR, peripheral smear, and differential count with automated high-precision analysers.

Thyroid Panel

Complete thyroid function testing — TSH, T3, T4, Free T3/T4 — with NABL-certified accuracy for precise diagnosis and monitoring.

ECG & Cardiac Tests

12-lead electrocardiogram with instant readings, lipid profile, troponin, and cardiac marker tests for comprehensive heart health screening.

Digital X-Ray

High-resolution digital X-ray imaging with minimal radiation exposure. Results reviewed by experienced radiologists within hours.

Diabetes Screening

Fasting glucose, post-prandial sugar, HbA1c, insulin resistance, and glucose tolerance tests for early detection and diabetes management.

Liver & Kidney Function

Complete hepatic and renal panels including LFT, KFT, GFR, creatinine, bilirubin, and uric acid for organ health assessment.

Urine Analysis

Routine and microscopic urinalysis, urine culture sensitivity, 24-hour protein, and microalbumin testing for comprehensive evaluation.

NABL-Certified Reports

Every report carries the NABL accreditation seal — internationally recognized quality assurance for accurate, reliable, and traceable results.
