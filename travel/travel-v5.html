How It Works

Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

Onboarding your company takes less than 48 hours. We handle the setup — you enjoy seamless travel management from day one.

1

Schedule a Discovery Call

We learn about your company's travel volume, preferred airlines/hotels, approval workflows, and compliance requirements. Takes just 30 minutes.

2

Custom Travel Policy Setup

Start Booking Effortlessly
