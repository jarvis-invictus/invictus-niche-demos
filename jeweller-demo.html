Our Process
From Design to Delivery
Every custom piece goes through our 5-step quality process to ensure you receive exactly what you envisioned.
1

Consultation & Design

Meet our design expert in-store or via WhatsApp video call. Share inspiration images, sketches, or describe your vision. We provide 3D CAD renders before any work begins.

2

Metal & Stone Selection

Choose your gold purity (18K, 22K, or 24K), colour (yellow/rose/white), and if diamonds are involved — select stones with GIA/IGI certificates reviewed together.

3

Master Karigar Crafting

Our in-house karigar with 25+ years of experience hand-crafts your piece. For complex bridal sets, allow 15–21 days for perfection.

4

Hallmarking & QC

Every finished piece is sent to BIS-accredited hallmarking centre. XRF testing confirms purity. Three-point quality check before packaging.

5

Handover & Invoice

Collect in-store or receive via insured home delivery. Full GST invoice with HUID, certificate, and care instructions included.
