How to Get Your Medicines Delivered

No app download needed. Just WhatsApp, call, or walk in — however suits you best.

1

Send Your Prescription

WhatsApp a photo of your prescription to us, or simply call and read out the medicine names. We accept prescriptions from any registered doctor.

2

We Verify & Prepare

Our pharmacist checks dosage, interactions, and availability. If something's off, we'll call your doctor directly — no back-and-forth for you.

3

Confirm & Pay

We send you a bill summary on WhatsApp. Pay via UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), card, or cash on delivery — whatever works for you.

4

Delivered to Your Door

Our delivery person reaches you within 2-3 hours (often sooner). Temperature-sensitive medicines come in insulated bags. It's that simple.
