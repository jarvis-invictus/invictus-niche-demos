Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery for medicines in Pune?
Yes, we deliver medicines across Pune including Kothrud, Baner, Wakad, and Hinjewadi. Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the same day. For recurring prescriptions, we offer auto-refill services so you never run out of essential medications.
Can I get a discount on medicines at your Kothrud pharmacy?
We offer 10 to 20 percent discount on most prescription medicines and OTC products. Senior citizens get an additional 5 percent off. We also have a loyalty program where regular customers earn points redeemable on future purchases at our Kothrud store.
Do you stock ayurvedic and homeopathic medicines?
Yes, we carry a wide range of ayurvedic, homeopathic, and wellness products alongside allopathic medicines. Brands like Himalaya, Patanjali, Dabur, and SBL are available. Our pharmacists can help you find alternatives if you prefer natural remedies for common ailments.
Are your pharmacists available for consultation?
Our qualified pharmacists are available during all operating hours to answer questions about medications, dosages, interactions, and side effects. They also help with understanding prescriptions and suggesting OTC alternatives when appropriate. We operate from 8 AM to 10 PM every day.
Can I upload my prescription online and pick up later?
Absolutely. You can WhatsApp your prescription to us or upload it on our website. We will keep your order ready for pickup within an hour. This saves time especially for busy professionals from the Kothrud, Deccan, and Paud Road areas.
