Health Corner

Seasonal Health Tips

Practical advice from our pharmacists to keep your family healthy year-round.

🌧️

Monsoon Immunity Boost

Waterborne infections spike during monsoons. Stock up on Vitamin C, zinc and ORS packets. Boil drinking water and avoid street food to prevent typhoid and gastroenteritis.

🗓️ June – September
☀️

Summer Hydration Guide

Pune summers can hit 40°C. Drink 3–4 liters daily, carry electrolyte sachets, apply SPF 30+ sunscreen. Watch for heat stroke signs — dizziness, nausea, rapid pulse.

🗓️ March – May
❄️

Winter Respiratory Care

Cold air aggravates asthma and allergies. Keep inhalers handy, use steam inhalation before bed, and get your Vitamin D levels checked — deficiency is common in winter.

🗓️ November – February
