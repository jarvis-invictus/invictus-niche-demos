Clinical Dispensing Services

Hospital-Grade Pharmacy Services
You Can Rely On

From inpatient ward orders to oncology infusion drugs — MedCore manages the full clinical medication lifecycle within the hospital ecosystem.

Priority Service

Discharge Medication Dispensing

Patients receiving hospital discharge summaries can fill all prescriptions instantly at MedCore — integrated with the ward nursing desk for zero-delay dispensing.

  • Same-floor pickup for discharge patients
  • Prescription cross-verified with treating physician
  • Take-home counselling from pharmacist
  • Refill scheduling for chronic medications
OPD Walk-in

OPD & Outpatient Prescriptions

Walk-in dispensing for all outpatient prescriptions from hospital-registered doctors. Quick, accurate, and fully counselled dispensing for every patient.

  • All specialty OPD prescriptions accepted
  • Generic substitution with doctor consent
  • Average dispensing time under 8 minutes
  • Prescription digitisation on request
Cold Chain

Cold Chain & Biologic Medicines

Temperature-sensitive biologics, insulin, vaccines, and specialty injectables are stored and dispensed under strict 2–8°C cold chain protocols with unbroken documentation.

  • Hospital-grade pharmaceutical refrigerators
  • Continuous temperature monitoring & alerts
  • Insulin, EPO, biologics, monoclonal antibodies
  • Cold pack packaging for home delivery
Speciality

Oncology & High-Risk Drug Dispensing

Chemotherapy support medicines, targeted therapy drugs, and high-alert medications dispensed by specialized oncology pharmacists with closed-system handling.

  • Chemotherapy oral agents & supportive meds
  • Closed-system handling for cytotoxic agents
  • Patient-specific dispensing with oncologist sign-off
  • Toxicity monitoring & counselling sessions

Our Commitments

Why Patients Trust
MedCore for Their Medicines

We exist because hospitals need pharmacies that operate with clinical-grade standards — not just retail-grade ones.

01
Registered Clinical Pharmacists On Duty
Our dispensing team is led by Pharm.D graduates registered with the Pharmacy Council of India. Every prescription is reviewed for drug interactions, dosage accuracy, and contraindications before dispensing.
02
Anti-Counterfeit Medicine Verification
Each medicine batch is authenticated via manufacturer's QR codes, holographic seals, and expiry verification. We source exclusively from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers and licensed stockists.
03
Post-Discharge Home Delivery
Patients who cannot return to the pharmacy after discharge can order refills via phone or WhatsApp. We deliver within Yerwada, Kalyani Nagar, Nagar Road, and surrounding Pune localities.
04
Seamless Hospital Billing Integration
For admitted patients, medicines can be billed directly to the hospital account. Cashless insurance claims are processed at the counter with no advance payment required for empanelled insurers.
For Discharged Patients

How Discharge Dispensing Works

1
Receive Discharge Summary
The ward nurse hands you your discharge summary with the prescription slip and pharmacy referral stamp.
2
Visit MedCore Pharmacy
Walk directly to MedCore on the ground floor — no need to go outside the hospital campus.
3
Pharmacist Verification
Our pharmacist verifies the prescription, checks for interactions, and prepares your medication pack with dosage labels.
4
Counselling & Dispensing
You receive a 5-minute medication counselling session, printed dosage schedule, and emergency contact card before leaving.
