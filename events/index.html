Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events does Celebrate Events manage in Pune?
We handle weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, product launches, conferences, and social gatherings across Pune. From intimate gatherings at Koregaon Park venues to grand celebrations at Hinjewadi convention centers, our team manages every detail from planning to execution.
How far in advance should I book your event planning services?
For weddings, we recommend booking three to six months ahead, especially during the November to February season. Corporate events and parties can be arranged with four to six weeks notice. For last-minute events, contact us directly and we will do our best to accommodate you.
Do you handle destination events outside Pune?
Yes, we regularly plan destination weddings and corporate retreats in Lonavala, Mahabaleshwar, Alibaug, and Goa. Our logistics team manages transportation, accommodation, and vendor coordination for outstation events. Pune-based clients love our seamless destination event management experience.
What is included in your event planning packages?
Our packages include venue selection, decor design, catering coordination, entertainment booking, photography and videography arrangements, guest management, and day-of coordination. We offer customizable packages from budget-friendly to premium, starting at ₹50,000 for small events.
Can you work within a specific budget for our Pune event?
Absolutely. Budget planning is one of our core strengths. We have vendor relationships across Pune that allow us to deliver impressive events at various price points. Whether it is a ₹2 lakh birthday or a ₹50 lakh wedding, we maximize value within your budget.
