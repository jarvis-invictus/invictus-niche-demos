What We Do

Full-Service Wedding Planning

From the first idea to the last dance — we handle every detail so you can be fully present on your big day.

Venue Scouting

We partner with 35+ premium venues across Pune & Maharashtra — from The Westin's grand ballrooms to Mahabaleshwar's hilltop estates. We negotiate the best rates and handle all bookings.

Décor & Design

Bespoke floral arrangements, mandap design, lighting architecture, and thematic styling. We transform spaces into dreamscapes — romantic pastels to regal jewel tones.

Catering Coordination

Curated menus from Pune's finest caterers. Multi-cuisine options, live counters, custom dietary planning, and signature cocktail bars. Tasting sessions included.

Photography & Film

Handpicked photographers and cinematographers who capture your story — pre-wedding shoots, candid moments, cinematic highlight reels, and same-day edits.

Day-of Management

A dedicated team of 4–8 coordinators on-site ensuring flawless execution. Vendor management, timeline enforcement, guest coordination, and emergency handling.

Logistics & Travel

Guest accommodation, airport transfers, valet parking, welcome hampers, and itinerary planning. Especially critical for destination weddings in Mahabaleshwar & Lonavala.

Our Process

How We Bring Your Vision to Life

A seamless four-step journey from "we're engaged!" to "best day ever."

01

Discovery Call

We listen to your vision, understand your style, and discuss budget. This free 45-minute consultation sets the foundation for everything.

02

Design & Plan

Our team creates a detailed mood board, shortlists vendors, proposes venues, and builds a comprehensive timeline and budget breakdown.

03

Refine & Rehearse

Tastings, venue walkthroughs, décor trials, and dress rehearsals. We fine-tune every element until it's exactly right. No detail overlooked.

04

Celebrate

Our on-site team manages the entire day. You enjoy every moment — from the baraat to the bidaai — while we handle everything behind the scenes.
