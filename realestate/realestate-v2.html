Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before leasing your next commercial space in Pune.

We cover the full commercial spectrum: Grade-A office spaces in IT parks (Hinjewadi, Kharadi, Magarpatta), retail showrooms and mall units, co-working and managed office solutions, warehousing, and commercial land. Whether you need 500 sq ft or an entire floor plate, we have inventory and relationships to match.
Most commercial leases in Pune run 3–9 years with a lock-in period of 1–3 years. Rent escalation is typically 5% per annum or renegotiated every 3 years. Co-working licenses tend to be shorter — 6 to 24 months with more flexibility. We negotiate terms that protect your interests, including exit clauses and fit-out amortization.
For tenants, our brokerage is typically one month's rent — industry standard in Pune. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. For large transactions or long-term leases, we often negotiate reduced or waived brokerage. Everything is disclosed upfront before you commit to a property visit.
Absolutely. We have a vetted network of interior designers, general contractors, IT infrastructure vendors, and furniture suppliers who specialize in commercial fit-outs. We facilitate introductions and can project-manage the setup if needed — from electrical and HVAC to workstation layout and branding.
