Why the Talegaon-Chakan Corridor?

The Pune-Nashik Industrial Corridor is Maharashtra's fastest-growing micro-market. With four Fortune 500 plants already operational in Chakan and a new logistics park under construction at Urse, land values here have outpaced both gold and FD returns for six consecutive years.

  • Compounding Appreciation — Without the Volatility Land here doesn't crash like equities. Scarcity of developable NA-converted plots is pushing prices 15–22% annually across the corridor. Your principal is protected by physical asset.
  • Industrial Demand Drives Residential Value Over 1,40,000 workers commute daily to Chakan MIDC. Housing demand keeps outstripping supply in the 15km radius — your land can be built upon or sold at a significant premium within 3–5 years.
  • Zero Maintenance, Full Control Unlike apartments, land costs you nothing each month. No society charges, no repairs, no tenants. Sit on it, build on it, or sell it — the decision is always yours.
  • Legally Air-Tight from Day One Every plot we list carries 7/12 extract verification, encumbrance certificate, and NA order copy. We engage your lawyer directly — no surprises at registration.
  • NRI-Ready Documentation Power of attorney drafting, repatriation compliance, FEMA documentation — our legal desk has handled 180+ NRI purchases from the US, UK, UAE, and Australia.

Simple 4-Step Process

From Discovery to Deed — We Handle Everything

Most of our clients close in under 45 days. Here's exactly what happens after you call us.

01
Discovery Call & Site Visit

Tell us your budget, purpose (residential/investment), and preferred zone. Within 24 hours we shortlist 3–5 matched plots and arrange a complimentary guided site visit — no pressure, no rush.

02
Legal Due Diligence

Our empanelled legal team runs a full title search — 30-year chain of ownership, 7/12 extract, city survey record, NA order, and encumbrance certificate. You receive a written diligence report before paying a single rupee.

03
Agreement & Token Amount

We draft the sale agreement in bilingual (Marathi-English) format, negotiate final price on your behalf, and facilitate a nominal token amount (usually 2–5%) to lock the plot. Balance timeline is flexible.

04
Registration & Possession

We accompany you to the sub-registrar's office in Talegaon, handle stamp duty calculation, and ensure physical possession with boundary demarcation on the same day as registration. Celebrate — you own land in Pune.
