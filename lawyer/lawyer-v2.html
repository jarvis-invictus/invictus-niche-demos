Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers to the questions we hear most from prospective clients.

What types of businesses do you typically work with?

We serve businesses across the growth spectrum — from funded startups and SMEs to mid-market companies and publicly listed corporations. Our core sectors include IT/SaaS, manufacturing, fintech, healthcare, and e-commerce. If your business has complex legal needs and values strategic thinking over commodity legal work, we're likely a good fit.

How does your pricing work? Do you bill by the hour?

We primarily use fixed-fee pricing for defined scopes of work — you'll always know the cost upfront before we begin. For ongoing advisory relationships, we offer monthly retainer packages that include a set number of hours, document reviews, and partner consultations. We believe unpredictable billing is a barrier to good legal counsel.

Can you handle cross-border transactions and foreign investment?

Absolutely. Cross-border work is one of our core competencies. We handle FDI structuring, FEMA compliance, overseas subsidiary setup, international joint ventures, and transfer pricing advisory. Adv. Deshmukh leads our cross-border practice and maintains relationships with partner firms in Singapore, the US, and the UK for seamless multi-jurisdiction coverage.

How quickly can you respond to urgent legal matters?

We guarantee a 24-hour response SLA for all active clients. For retainer clients, urgent matters receive same-day attention — often within 2-4 hours. We understand that business doesn't wait for legal opinions, so we've built our practice around speed without sacrificing thoroughness.

What does the initial consultation look like?

Your first consultation is a complimentary 30-minute session with a partner — not a junior associate. We'll discuss your business, understand your legal needs, identify immediate risks, and outline a strategic approach. There's no obligation. If we're the right fit, we'll provide a clear scope of work and fixed-fee proposal within 48 hours.
