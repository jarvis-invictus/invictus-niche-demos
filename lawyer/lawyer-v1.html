Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you have many questions during such a difficult time. Here are answers to the ones we hear most often.

How long does a mutual consent divorce take in Pune?

Under the Hindu Marriage Act, a mutual consent divorce typically takes 6–18 months from the date of filing. There is a mandatory 6-month cooling-off period after the first motion, though courts can waive this in certain circumstances. We help streamline the process and prepare all documentation in advance to avoid unnecessary delays.

What are my rights regarding child custody?

Both parents have equal rights to seek custody. Courts primarily consider the child's welfare and best interests. For children under 5, courts often (but not always) favour the mother. We build strong cases based on the parent's involvement, stability, and the child's emotional and educational needs.

How is maintenance (alimony) calculated?

There is no fixed formula. Courts consider multiple factors: the spouse's income and assets, the dependent spouse's needs and earning capacity, the standard of living during marriage, the duration of marriage, and children's needs. We meticulously document financial evidence to secure the best possible maintenance award.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely. Your first consultation — whether in person, over the phone, or via WhatsApp — is completely free and confidential. We believe everyone deserves to understand their legal options before making any commitments. There is no pressure and no obligation to proceed.

Can you help with domestic violence protection orders urgently?

Yes. Domestic violence cases are treated with the highest urgency. Under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, we can seek emergency interim relief and protection orders, often within 48–72 hours of filing. Your safety is our absolute priority.

What documents do I need for a court marriage?

For registration under the Special Marriage Act, you'll need: age proof (birth certificate/10th marksheet), address proof (Aadhaar/passport), passport-size photographs, an affidavit of marital status, and three witnesses with ID proof. We handle all paperwork, notice publication, and court coordination for you.
