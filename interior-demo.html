How It Works

Our Design Process

A structured, collaborative process that keeps you informed at every stage — no surprises.

01
Discovery Call
We understand your vision, lifestyle, and requirements. Free 45-minute consultation.
02
Concept Design
Mood boards, layout plans, material palettes, and reference imagery to align on the aesthetic direction.
03
3D Visualization
Photorealistic renders of every room so you can approve the design before execution begins.
04
Execution
Civil work, carpentry, painting, electrical — all coordinated by our project manager. Weekly progress updates.
05
Handover
Final walkthrough, snag resolution, and complete documentation. Your dream space, delivered on time.
