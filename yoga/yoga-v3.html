Our Story

Where silk meets soul

Sky & Soul was born from a simple idea: that flying should feel like coming home. We created a space where you can let go — of stress, of gravity, of everything that keeps you grounded when you'd rather soar.

Nestled on the third floor of a sunlit Koregaon Park lane house, our studio features 14 professional-grade silk hammocks, mood lighting, and a curated soundscape that makes every session feel like a ritual. Whether you're healing an old injury, building core strength, or simply chasing that upside-down rush — we've got a hammock waiting for you.

First Time?

What to expect on your first flight

Nervous? Good — that means something exciting is about to happen. Here's everything you need to know before you show up.

👕

Wear the right stuff

Fitted clothing that covers your armpits and the backs of your knees. No zippers, no jewellery, no lotions. The silk needs to grip — and so do you.

🕐

Arrive 15 mins early

Your first session includes a quick orientation: hammock safety, studio etiquette, and adjusting your silk to the right height. We've got you.

💪

No fitness level required

Seriously. We've had 70-year-olds and teenagers in the same class. The hammock supports up to 300 kg — it'll hold you, we promise.

🍽️

Eat light, 2 hours before

You'll be upside down at some point. A heavy meal and inversions aren't best friends. Light snack is fine — full biryani is not.

🤸

Expect to surprise yourself

Most first-timers are flying within 20 minutes. That moment when the hammock takes your weight and you let go — it's unforgettable.

📸

Yes, it's Instagrammable

We have a dedicated photo corner with ring lights and hammock drapes. Post-class content is practically a studio tradition. Tag us!
