Your Therapist

Dr. Meera Kulkarni,
C-IAYT, MSc Yoga Therapy

After witnessing her mother's struggle with chronic back pain, Dr. Meera dedicated her career to understanding the intersection of yoga and medicine. Trained at SVYASA University and certified by the International Association of Yoga Therapists, she brings a deeply personal approach to every session.

At Healing Roots, we don't believe in pushing through pain. We believe in listening to your body, understanding its language, and creating space for it to heal — gently, on its own terms.

  • 🎓MSc Yoga Therapy — SVYASA University, Bangalore
  • 📋Certified Yoga Therapist (C-IAYT) — International Association of Yoga Therapists
  • 🏥Clinical Training — KEM Hospital Pune, Pain Management Dept.
  • 🧠PG Diploma in Applied Psychology & Counselling
  • 🤝Consultant to 4 physiotherapy clinics across PCMC

"Healing isn't about bending further — it's about breathing deeper. I meet every student exactly where they are, not where they think they should be."

What We Treat

Conditions We Work With

Therapeutic yoga isn't a replacement for medical care — it's a powerful companion. We work alongside your doctors, physiotherapists, and psychologists.

🦴

Chronic Back & Neck Pain

Targeted sequences for spinal decompression, gentle stretching, and strengthening the support muscles that protect your back.

🦵

Joint & Knee Issues

Chair-assisted and prop-supported yoga for arthritis, post-operative recovery, and degenerative joint conditions.

😰

Anxiety & Panic Disorders

Breathing-first protocols, vagus nerve activation, and grounding techniques that calm the nervous system from the inside out.

😴

Insomnia & Sleep Disorders

Yoga Nidra, progressive relaxation, and evening wind-down sequences proven to improve sleep onset and quality.

🩹

Post-Surgical Recovery

Careful, phased rehabilitation yoga for hip replacements, spinal surgeries, and other post-operative healing journeys.

🧠

Stress & Burnout

Restorative sessions for overwhelmed professionals — slow movement, long holds, and permission to just… rest.

🫁

Respiratory Conditions

Pranayama-focused therapy for asthma, COPD, and post-COVID breathing rehabilitation with measurable progress tracking.

🤰

Prenatal & Postnatal Care

Safe, trimester-appropriate yoga and postnatal recovery designed with OB-GYN guidance.

Our Programs

Classes Designed to Heal

Every class is small (max 8 students), prop-supported, and paced for comfort — not performance. No one gets left behind.

All Levels

Gentle Restore

Our signature restorative class. Floor-based poses held for 5–8 minutes each, fully supported by bolsters, blankets, and blocks. Perfect for nervous system regulation.

Pain Management Program
