Our Classes

Six Paths, One Journey

From energising morning Vinyasa to restorative evening Yin — there's a practice here that fits your life, your body, and where you are right now.

Foundation

Hatha Yoga

The classical practice. Slower pace, longer holds, precise alignment. Perfect for building a strong, mindful foundation — or returning to basics after time away.

⏱ 75 min 🧘 All Levels
Flow

Vinyasa Flow

Breath-linked movement that builds heat, strength, and rhythm. Our most popular morning class — the one that gets KP professionals through their Monday.

⏱ 60 min 🧘 Beginner–Intermediate
Restore

Yin Yoga

Deep, quiet, and surprisingly intense. Long-held floor poses that release fascia, calm the nervous system, and teach you to sit with what is. A perfect counterbalance to screen-heavy days.

⏱ 75 min 🧘 All Levels
Breathwork

Pranayama & Meditation

Where the real transformation happens. Structured breathwork followed by guided meditation — from Nadi Shodhana to Kapalabhati. Our quietest class and, many say, our most powerful.

⏱ 45 min 🧘 All Levels
Strength

Power Yoga

Athletically demanding, sweat-inducing, and deeply satisfying. For those who want to build muscle, challenge their edge, and still walk away with a clear mind.

⏱ 60 min 🧘 Intermediate–Advanced
Corporate

Corporate Wellness

We bring the studio to your office — or your team to ours. Tailored sessions for IT parks, startups, and co-working spaces across Pune. Desk-friendly yoga that actually helps.

⏱ 45–60 min 🧘 All Levels

Our Teachers

Guided by Experience, Grounded in Practice

Every teacher at Prana Life has a daily personal practice. They teach not from textbooks, but from years on the mat — and a genuine desire to see you grow.

Ananya Kulkarni

Lead Vinyasa & Pranayama

Born and raised in Pune, Ananya found yoga after burning out in the IT industry at 26. Twelve years later, she leads our teacher training programme and has studied with masters in Mysore and Rishikesh. Her classes are challenging, warm, and always soundtracked by a genuine laugh.

E-RYT 500 · YACEP · Pranayama Specialist

Rohan Deshmukh

Power Yoga & Vinyasa

Former national-level swimmer turned yoga teacher. Rohan brings athletic precision and playful energy to every class. He's the reason half our students can hold a handstand — and the reason the other half keep trying. Also runs our Corporate Wellness outreach.

RYT 500 · Functional Movement · Sports Yoga

Priya Nair

Yin Yoga & Prenatal

Priya's voice alone is enough to lower your cortisol. A former physiotherapist from Kerala who relocated to Pune, she specialises in therapeutic yoga, prenatal care, and helping people recover from injury. Her Yin classes have a three-month waitlist.

RYT 500 · RPYT · Yoga Therapy Certified

Meera Joshi

Founder & Hatha Teacher

Meera founded Prana Life in 2018 after two decades of practice and a realisation that Pune deserved a yoga space that felt like home, not a gym. She teaches Hatha with the patience of someone who's fallen out of every pose — and the wisdom of someone who got back up.

E-RYT 500 · Iyengar Trained · 20+ Years Practice
