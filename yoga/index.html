Our Story

Born in Kothrud,
Rooted in Tradition

Founded in 2016 by Jigar and Meera Rao, Aura began as a single morning class on a terrace with 12 students. Today, we are Kothrud's most loved yoga studio — with over 600 active members and 8 experienced instructors.

We blend classical Hatha and Iyengar traditions with modern approaches like Power Yoga and therapeutic classes for specific conditions. Whether you're 20 or 65, a beginner or a daily practitioner, there's a class for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What styles of yoga does Aura Yoga teach in Pune?
We offer Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Yin, and Restorative yoga along with guided meditation and pranayama sessions. Whether you are a complete beginner or advanced practitioner, our certified instructors customize their approach. Morning and evening batches run at our peaceful Kothrud studio.
How much do yoga classes cost at Aura Yoga Kothrud?
Monthly unlimited passes start at ₹2,500 and drop-in classes are ₹400 each. We offer quarterly and annual packages with significant discounts. Student and senior citizen rates are available too. Your first trial class is free so you can experience our teaching style before committing.
Do I need to be flexible to start yoga classes?
Not at all. That is like saying you need to be clean to take a shower. Our beginner-friendly classes start with foundational poses and gradually build flexibility and strength. Many of our students from Warje and Kothrud started with zero flexibility and now practice advanced poses.
Do you offer online yoga classes as well?
Yes, we run live online sessions via Zoom for students who cannot make it to our Kothrud studio. These are interactive classes with real-time corrections, not just recorded videos. Students from Hinjewadi, PCMC, and even other cities join our online morning batches.
What should I bring for my first yoga class at Aura?
Just wear comfortable clothing and bring a water bottle. We provide yoga mats, blocks, and straps at the studio. Arrive 10 minutes early to fill in a quick health form and settle in. Our Kothrud studio has changing rooms, lockers, and a calm waiting area for your comfort.
