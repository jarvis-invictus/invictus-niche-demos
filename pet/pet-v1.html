Why Pet Parents Love Us

The Paws & Polish Promise

100% Pet-Safe Products

We use only vet-recommended, sulphate-free, non-toxic shampoos and conditioners. Safe for sensitive skin and puppies too.

Certified Groomers

Our team is trained at accredited grooming academies and attends annual workshops to stay current with breed standards.

Progress Photos Sent to You

We WhatsApp you a mid-session update so you can see your fur baby mid-groom. Because separation anxiety hits both ways!

Hand-Dried, Never Cage-Dried

Every pet is hand-dried with a warm, low-noise dryer. No scary cage dryers. Your pet's comfort is our non-negotiable.

Vet Tie-Up Next Door

Our partner vet clinic is just a two-minute walk away in Viman Nagar for immediate medical support if ever needed.

On-Time Pickup, Every Time

We respect your schedule. Get an SMS when your pet is ready — usually within the estimated window, never kept waiting.
