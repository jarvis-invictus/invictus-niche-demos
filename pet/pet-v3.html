FAQ

Got Questions?

We've answered the most common ones here — ask us anything via WhatsApp!

All dogs must be up to date on DHLPPi (Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza) and Rabies vaccines. Cats need FVRCP and Rabies. Please carry your pet's vaccination card on check-in day. Pets due for renewal within 2 weeks are accepted on a case-by-case basis.
Absolutely! We encourage you to bring your pet's regular food to avoid stomach upsets. Bringing a familiar blanket, toy, or t-shirt (with your scent) also helps your pet settle in faster. We provide food and treats for all packages, but your pet's own food is always welcome.
After check-in, we send you a secure link via WhatsApp. You can watch live HD feeds of the play area, hallways, and common areas. Private rooms have in-room cameras accessible only to parents of pets in that room. Feeds are available from 7 AM to 10 PM daily. We also send WhatsApp video updates 2-3 times/day.
We carefully assess all dogs before group play. Dogs with a history of aggression or resource guarding are accommodated in our individual care program — they still get playtime, but with a 1-on-1 caretaker in a separate zone. Safety of all our furry guests is our top priority. Please disclose behavioural history at the time of booking.
Check-in: 8 AM – 11 AM or 5 PM – 7 PM. Check-out: 8 AM – 12 PM. Late check-out (up to 3 PM) is available for ₹150 extra. Early drop-off before 8 AM can be arranged by prior appointment. For daycare, drop-off is 7:30 AM – 10 AM and pick-up is 5 PM – 8 PM.
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in — full refund. Cancellations 24-48 hours before: 50% refund. Less than 24 hours: no refund, but you can reschedule within 30 days. We understand emergencies happen — reach out via WhatsApp and we'll always try to work with you.
