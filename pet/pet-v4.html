Our Story

Born Out of a
Golden Retriever's Demands

It started with Bruno — our founder Priya's stubborn, loveable golden retriever who turned his nose up at every ordinary collar, bowl, and bed in the market. Priya searched all of Pune and found nothing that met her standards.

So in 2018, she opened PawBoutique on a quiet lane in Koregaon Park with just 40 handpicked products and a promise: every pet deserves the same care and quality their human expects.

Today, we stock over 300 products, serve 2,400+ happy pets across Pune, and ship pan-India. Bruno still approves every new arrival personally.
