Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need a referral to book a consultation?
No referral needed. You can book directly online, by phone, or walk in. First consultations include a full skin examination and diagnosis.
How long does a consultation take?
First consultations are 40–50 minutes. Follow-up appointments are 20–30 minutes. We don't rush — skin takes time to assess properly.
Is laser treatment safe for Indian skin tones?
Yes — with the right settings and equipment. We specialize in Fitzpatrick skin types IV–VI and have protocols specifically developed for darker skin tones.
Are your procedures covered by insurance?
Medical dermatology consultations and prescriptions are generally covered. Cosmetic procedures (laser, fillers, etc.) are not. We can provide detailed bills for reimbursement claims.
