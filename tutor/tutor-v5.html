What We Teach

Mediums & Techniques

Master the full spectrum of fine arts — from delicate watercolours to bold sculptures.

Watercolour

Wet-on-wet, dry brush, glazing & lifting techniques for luminous art

Acrylic

Versatile & vibrant — palette knife, impasto, layering & blending

Oil Painting

Classical techniques — alla prima, sfumato, chiaroscuro & glazing

Charcoal & Graphite

Value studies, portraiture, figure drawing & expressive mark-making

Sculpture & Clay

Hand-building, coiling, slab work, and basic wheel-throwing

Crafts & Mixed Media

Collage, printmaking, textile art, resin crafts & assemblage
