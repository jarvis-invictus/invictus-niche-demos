Beginner to Advanced

A Clear Path From First Note to Mastery

Every musician's journey is unique. Our structured curriculum ensures steady progression while honouring your individual pace and goals.

I

Foundation

Perfect for absolute beginners — build confidence and core skills.

  • Basic music theory & notation
  • Posture, breathing & technique
  • Simple compositions & exercises
  • Introduction to ragas / scales
  • Monthly progress assessments
II

Intermediate

Deepen your artistry — explore complex pieces and improvisation.

  • Advanced raga / chord exploration
  • Taal patterns & time signatures
  • Ensemble playing & collaboration
  • Stage performance preparation
  • Exam & competition readiness
III

Advanced

Master-level refinement — prepare for professional performance.

  • Complex compositions & jugalbandi
  • Improvisation & creative expression
  • Recording & production basics
  • Concert & recital preparation
  • Mentorship with guest artists
