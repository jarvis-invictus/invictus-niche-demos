Our Proven Methodology

A structured, research-backed approach that has helped 500+ students across Pune achieve their target band scores consistently.

Diagnostic Assessment

Every student begins with a full-length diagnostic test. We identify strengths and weaknesses across all modules and create a personalised study roadmap with clear milestones.

Structured Curriculum

Cambridge and British Council aligned materials. Each week follows clear progression — vocabulary, grammar mastery, strategy drilling, and full mock simulations.

Weekly Mock Tests

Writing Clinic
