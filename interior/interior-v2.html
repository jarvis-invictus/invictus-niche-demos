Our Process

From Brief to Handover in 45 Days

A transparent, milestone-driven process that keeps your project on track and your team informed at every stage.

01

Discovery & Site Survey

We visit your space, understand your team size, workflows, brand guidelines, and compliance requirements. You receive a detailed site report within 48 hours.

02

Design & 3D Visualization

Our architects create CAD layouts, 3D renders, and material mood boards. You approve every detail — from ceiling grid patterns to desk-leg finishes — before we break ground.

03

Execution & Quality Control

Dedicated project manager on-site daily. Weekly progress reports with photos. Strict 47-point quality checklist at every milestone. Zero surprises.

04

Handover & AMC Support

Deep cleaning, final walkthrough, snag-list resolution, and keys. We stay on as your facility partner with optional 12-month AMC coverage.
