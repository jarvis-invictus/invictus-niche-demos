How We Work

From Vision to Reality in 4 Simple Steps

We've streamlined our process so you get a stunning home without the usual chaos. Here's exactly what to expect.

01

Free Consultation

We visit your home, understand your lifestyle, preferences, and budget. No pressure, no commitment — just an honest conversation about possibilities.

02

Design Concept

Our team creates 3D renders and mood boards so you can see your space before a single nail is hammered. We iterate until you say "that's perfect."

03

Execution

Our in-house team handles everything — carpentry, electrical, painting, and installations. Weekly updates keep you in the loop without the stress.

04

Handover & Care

We do a final walkthrough together, make sure every detail is right, and hand you the keys to your dream home. Plus, 2 years of after-service support.

Our Story

Built on the Belief That Every Home Deserves Great Design

SpaceStory Interiors was born in 2017 when founder Neha Desai noticed something broken in Pune's interior design industry: beautiful work was reserved for luxury budgets, while middle-class families were stuck choosing between affordability and aesthetics.

Neha — a graduate of Rachana Sansad with 3 years at a top Mumbai design firm — returned to Pune with a mission: make thoughtful, personalised interior design accessible to every family moving into their dream home.

Today, SpaceStory is a team of 12 designers, project managers, and craftsmen who've transformed over 150 homes across Baner, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Aundh, and Pashan. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all design. We believe in sitting with you, understanding how you live, and creating spaces that feel unmistakably yours.

Our promise: transparent pricing, on-time delivery, and a home you'll fall in love with — every single day.

— Neha Desai, Founder
