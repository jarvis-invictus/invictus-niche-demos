Featured Project

The Kalyani Residence

Kalyani Nagar, Pune

A 6,200 sq ft bungalow reimagined as a gallery of living — Italian Calacatta marble flows through the double-height foyer into a sequence of rooms, each choreographed for light and intimacy. Hand-forged bronze accents, bespoke walnut joinery from our Jaipur workshop, and silk wall coverings from a 200-year-old Varanasi atelier. The living pavilion opens onto a landscaped courtyard framed by century-old rain trees, blurring the boundary between architecture and nature.

Penthouse

The Baner Penthouse

Baner, Pune

Perched on the 28th floor with uninterrupted views of the Sahyadri mountain range, this 4,800 sq ft duplex penthouse is a study in aerial luxury. Nero Marquina marble grounds the space with dramatic veining, while brushed brass fixtures catch the shifting western light. A floating staircase in blackened steel and glass connects the social floor to the private quarters above. The kitchen — clad in honed Statuario — doubles as a sculptural centrepiece for the homeowner's legendary dinner parties.

Nero Marquina Brushed Brass Blackened Steel Statuario Smoked Oak
Area 4,800 sq ft
Duration 11 Months
Investment ₹1.9 Crore
Typology Penthouse
Estate

The Boat Club Estate

Boat Club Road, Pune

A private farmhouse on Pune's most prestigious address, restored with the reverence it deserves. Reclaimed Burmese teak floors — each plank with a provenance story — anchored by handwoven Kashmiri carpets sourced directly from Srinagar's master weavers. Museum-grade Erco lighting illuminates a curated art collection spanning three generations. The formal dining room seats 18 beneath a custom chandelier of hand-blown Murano glass. Heritage architecture meets contemporary comfort — timeless, unapologetic luxury that honours the past while embracing the future.

Burmese Teak Kashmiri Carpets Murano Glass Sandstone Belgian Linen
Area 8,500 sq ft
Duration 18 Months
Investment ₹4.2 Crore

Our Philosophy

The Art of
Considered Living

"Luxury is not about excess — it is about the discipline of selecting only what is extraordinary, and allowing it room to breathe." — Founding Principle, Elara Living

At Elara Living, we believe every material tells a story, every proportion evokes an emotion, and every home should be an irreplaceable work of architecture. We don't follow trends — we establish them. Our design language draws from the quiet confidence of old-world craftsmanship and the clean precision of contemporary thought.

Material Integrity

Only the finest — sourced from quarries in Carrara, workshops in Jaipur, foundries in Milan, and ateliers in Kyoto.

Spatial Poetry

Every room is choreographed for light, movement, and the rituals of daily life — designed to feel inevitable.

Bespoke Always

No templates, no repetition. Each commission begins with a blank page and the client's singular story.

Enduring Design

We design for decades, not seasons. Timelessness over trendiness, substance over spectacle, always.

Our Approach

From Vision to Reality

A refined four-phase journey that transforms your aspirations into a living masterpiece — with complete transparency at every stage.

I

Discovery

An intimate consultation to understand your lifestyle, aesthetic sensibilities, and the emotional quality you seek in your home. We listen before we draw.

II

Design

Immersive 3D visualizations, material mood boards, and detailed drawings — presented at our studio or your residence for your review and refinement.

III

Craft

Our atelier and trusted artisan network bring the design to life with exacting precision. Weekly progress updates and site inspections ensure perfection.

IV

Reveal

The culminating moment — a styled, fully finished home delivered to the last detail. From art placement to scent selection, every element considered.
