Frequently Asked Questions

What advanced laser technologies does ClearSkin use?
We use modern equipment including fractional CO2 lasers, Q-switched Nd:YAG for pigmentation, and diode lasers for hair removal. Our Koregaon Park clinic also features digital dermoscopy for precise diagnosis. All devices are US FDA-approved and calibrated for Indian skin types.
Can I get acne surgery at ClearSkin Advanced Dermatology?
Yes, we perform acne surgery for stubborn comedones and cystic lesions that do not respond to medication alone. The procedure is minimally invasive and done under local anaesthesia. Patients from Mundhwa, Viman Nagar, and Camp visit us regularly for this specialized treatment.
Does ClearSkin Advanced offer anti-aging and cosmetic procedures?
Absolutely. Our cosmetic dermatology services include Botox, dermal fillers, thread lifts, PRP for hair and skin, and laser skin tightening. We focus on natural-looking results with minimal downtime, perfect for busy professionals across Pune who want effective treatments.
How do I book an appointment at the Koregaon Park clinic?
You can book online through our website, call our reception, or message us on WhatsApp. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening slots to suit your schedule. The clinic is conveniently located in Koregaon Park with easy parking and access from MG Road.
What is the recovery time after laser treatments at ClearSkin?
Recovery varies by procedure. Surface-level peels require one to two days of mild redness, while fractional lasers may need five to seven days of downtime. Our team provides detailed aftercare instructions and follow-up support to ensure smooth healing and optimal results.
