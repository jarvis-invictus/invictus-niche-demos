What aesthetic treatments does LuminaSkin offer in Baner?

Is laser skin resurfacing at LuminaSkin safe for Indian skin tones?
Absolutely. Our dermatologists are specifically trained in treating Indian skin types (Fitzpatrick III-V). We use calibrated laser settings and post-procedure protocols designed for melanin-rich skin. Patients from Balewadi and Pashan trust us for safe, effective results without hyperpigmentation risk.
