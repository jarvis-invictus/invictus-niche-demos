How affordable are consultations at DermaCare in Kothrud?
Our consultations start at just 350, making quality dermatology accessible to everyone. We believe good skin care should not be expensive. Patients from Warje, Karve Nagar, and Bavdhan regularly visit us for affordable yet effective skin treatments without compromising on quality.
Does DermaCare accept health insurance for skin treatments?
Yes, we accept most major health insurance providers for covered dermatological procedures. Our team helps with paperwork and pre-authorization so you can focus on your treatment. Walk-in patients without insurance also benefit from our affordable pricing at the Kothrud clinic.
What common skin problems does DermaCare treat?
We treat acne, pigmentation, hair fall, fungal infections, eczema, psoriasis, warts, and more. Our doctors at the Kothrud and Hadapsar locations focus on practical, proven treatments. We also offer preventive skincare advice tailored to Pune's humid climate and urban pollution.
Can I get same-day appointments at DermaCare Kothrud?
Yes, we keep slots open for same-day and walk-in patients every day except Sundays. For working professionals, we offer evening consultations until 8 PM. You can also book online for a specific time slot to avoid waiting at our Kothrud clinic.
Is DermaCare suitable for children's skin problems?
Definitely. Our dermatologists are experienced in pediatric skin conditions like eczema, rashes, and fungal infections. We use gentle, child-safe treatments and take extra care to make young patients comfortable. Many families from Kothrud and surrounding areas trust us for their children's skin health.
